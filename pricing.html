Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

How does the 14-day free trial work?

Start using Freytor immediately with full access to all features of your chosen plan. No credit card required. After 14 days, choose to continue with a paid subscription or cancel with no charges.

What happens if I exceed my shipment limit?

You'll be notified when approaching your limit. Additional shipments are charged at $0.10 per shipment on Starter, $0.08 on Growth. Enterprise has unlimited shipments included.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades apply at the next billing cycle, and you keep access to your current features until then.

Which carriers do you integrate with?

We support 100+ carriers including DHL, FedEx, UPS, local couriers, and regional carriers across emerging markets. Custom carrier integrations available on Enterprise plans.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?

Yes! We offer special pricing for verified non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team to learn more about eligibility and discounts.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transfers. Your data is stored in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centers with daily backups and 99.9% uptime guarantee.
